Kailua

  • Lanikai Beach

  • Beach

  • Map

Considered to be one of the most beautiful beaches on Oahu, so much so that it's nicknamed Heavenly Beach.
The water is usually super calm so you can have a great time enjoying the sun and swimming near the beach.
There is no designated parking area so you will have to find open street parking, should be easier to find if you go on the weekdays.

North Shore

  • Waimea Bay

  • Beach

  • Map

Waimea Bay is a must-visit if you're in the North Shore especially in the Summer. You will see a drastic change depending on the season.
During the winter time, the waves are huge. Summer time is much calmer with no waves and the big rock near the beach is a popular jumping spot.

One of the most famous shave ice shops on the island. If you're in the North Shore and get a chance to go with a sweet tooth, I recommend checking this shop out.
